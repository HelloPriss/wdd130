Utah

Utah is a state in the western United States, known for its diverse geography that includes mountains, deserts, and national parks. It is home to five national parks, often referred to as the "Mighty 5," which include Zion, Bryce Canyon, Arches, Canyonlands, and Capitol Reef. Utah is also known for its rich history, including Native American heritage and Mormon settlements.
